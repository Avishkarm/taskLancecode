Signing Up

Signing up to TaskLance is free and can be done here Only registered users may post and get on TaskLance. After successfully signing up, you shall want to check your registration email for the activation link provided in a welcome email.

For Clients

You can browse the homepage, category or subcategory pages, or use the Post a task to connect a TaskLancer for the task in your hand. Once you find a receive choice of TaskLancers, view the TaskLancer rating and availability just proceed to checkout. You have several ways to pay for the service.

For TaskLancers

Clients pay TaskLance for tasks in advance. TaskLancer keep 90% of each task they get and successfully deliver. For example, you get a Task of estimate 5000 and successfully deliver, accredits your account with a net revenue of 4500. TaskLance accredits clients once an order is completed.
