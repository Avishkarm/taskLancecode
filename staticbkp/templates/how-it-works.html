How Pricing Works?

Pricing for tasks are fixed on our platform. Task rates are non-negotiable and by submitting a Task, you are accepting listed rate. Our platform is No Bidding platform; so a quote or bidding cannot be directly provided for your task. In order to find the various prices for Tasks, you will need to enter in the information for your task. It is important to indicate details such as the task category, task subcategory, so we can filter your task results to accurately display recommended TaskLancers. Payment for your task is strictly through the TaskLance website. If you have further questions regarding this, please do not hesitate to email us.

When TaskLancers are Available?

Mostly Recommended TaskLancers are available to respond immediately as we show only online freelancers. But if they are not online, you can do “offline hiring”. Browse offline TaskLancers, you will still have the option to send a task invitation. When hiring a TaskLancers who is offline, you will be shown the amount of time that has elapsed since that TaskLancers was last online. The purpose of this is to give you an accurate idea of whether or not this TaskLancers is actively using the platform. When booking a task for a future date, you will be asked to select date. Taskers are also known to coordinate "Off Hours" for early morning or late night tasks. To coordinate this, simply include these details in your description when booking your task. For example, include information such as "I'll need assistance from 6 p.m. - 11 p.m." so that it's clear when you need assistance. Your TaskLancers will then coordinate with you directly to confirm the details of your task!
