Project by Good Neighbor Multipurpose Educational Trust

  • OASIS INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL benefits with the intent to uplift the rural populace by improving women’s health education and providing schooling for the young boys and girls of the community. They provide education from kindergarten to the 12th grade.

Work done so far

  • Infrastructure of 5 acres of land for OASIS INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL.
  • School building with independent water supply and lavatories.
  • 6 school buses
  • 80 girl children and 150 boy children studing from nursery to 7th grade
